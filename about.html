About us

VendorsI.com

Established in 2024, VendoesI is an Egyptian e-commerce and retail company. Through our mobile application, we connect honest sellers with honest buyers.

Vision

Empowering youth vendors to thrive through organized and simplified selling experiences. Our app is dedicated to fostering community support; by providing a seamless platform for emerging vendors to showcase their products. For our clients, we are committed to curating a one-stop shop for useful products. With our guarantees and customer-friendly refund policies, we aim to redefine convenience, and ensure a hassle-free shopping experience for everyone.

Mission

Our mission is to revolutionize the way youth vendors operate and maximize their assets through e-commerce.

Our mission is to curate a vibrant marketplace where they can discover the products they need, and get all services in one place.
